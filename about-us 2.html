Ntshanga Capital was established in 2016 and began trading the same year. Our main focus is facilitation and development process of software products until they are ready for commercialization.

We are committed to meeting the needs of users.We work according to goals and pride ourselves with delivery. We work according to time frames unless urgent circumstances force us to renegotiate dead line.

We take full responsibility of the work that we are required to do. We strive in uniqueness and creativity, our products are designed to be clearly distinguished from others in the market. Accountability creates loyalty from our users and establishing assists in building long term relationships with them.

We work with integrity we are open about what we can bring to the table, our capabilities and level of knowledge we have about any project we deal with. Mutual trust is earned in workplace through completion of task/ duties assigned to a member, with customers trust is built through quality service delivery.
